This is more than just a persona project It is life safe for future generation and one of the keys to sustainable development of Africa.

I present to you canopy loss monitor AI; A Python-based AI tool that uses Machine Learning (ML) to analyze satellite images. 
It uses a Classification Model to automatically detect tree loss and provide data-driven alerts to help prevent deforestation in real-time.

To come up with this idea I analysed the natural ecosystemof Rwanda primarily and Africa as well as fuel categories with their respective 
consumption and I found a huge mostly ignored brutal truth.Roughly 80% to 94% of Rwandan households depend on wood fuel (firewood and charcoal)
for cookingalso sadly;up to 94% of the rural population in some African regions uses wood fuel as their primary energy source.and why is that 
unexpectedly It is not that renewable methods or fuels areall expensive but the abundance of weakly guarded forest in Africa or in Rwanda where by
you find them large and often contaning dangerous aspects making It harder to guard them. So in order to fill this gap in the system I came with
canopy loss AI as a solution to help the government curb deforestation and charcoal burning without barriers and with minimal human intervention
allowing maximum guard no matter the place nor the conditions.

conclusively, canopy loss AI is not just a didital guard It is a revolutionally solution and upon completion and implementation ; there will be significant
reduction in in deforestation, charcoal burning and drastic switches of households to to sustainable sources of fuel hence aligning with the world's vision 
of sustainability.
